About Toyota Marilao

A brainchild of the 3 Oben Group Toyota dealerships, TOYOTA MARILAO is the first of its kind to house economy and ecology under one roof. It is the future of the automotive industry, an ecological benchmark that sets new standards for environmental sustainability and the promotion of green initiatives. Incorporating renewable resources, particularly solar energy through innovative yet environmental friendly technology gives Toyota Marilao an edge above the rest.

By far, our facilities have the lowest carbon footprint among all car dealerships in the country, thus providing a very earth friendly environment for our customers and our people.

Atty. Reginaldo A. Oben

Serves as Chairman of the Oben Group of Toyota Dealers comprising of Toyota Balintawak, Toyota Otis, Toyota North EDSA, Toyota Marilao, Bulacan, and Toyota San Jose, Del Monte, Bulacan (the “Oben Group”).

While originally engaged in the shipping business, being the President and Chairman of Wallem Philippines Shipping Inc., one of the largest Ship Agency in the country, Atty. Oben’sstint in the automotive dealership industry started when the local automotive company, Toyota Motor Philippines decided to appoint him, among many contenders, to be the Toyota dealership franchise owner in the northern portion of Metro Manila in 1989. His excellent credentials, strong business acumen, technical know-how, and utmost credibility paved the way for the beginning of Atty. Oben’s venture into the car dealership industry through Toyota Balintawak.

Location

Located at L1505 McArthur Highway, Abangan Sur, Marilao, Bulacan, Toyota Marilao as a member of the Oben Group boasts of a 15,000 square meter state-of-the-art facility, enhanced even more by several environment-friendly technology. Its special heat-resistant canopies keep customers comfortable while reducing electricity consumption by 30% and an impressive line of power-generating solar panels have been installed for renewable energy to be utilized by all departments; Thus doubling its efficiency without depleting more resources.

Strategically situated in a fast-growing industrial area, it is near landmarks like SM Marilao and other commercial establishments that pave the way for the town’s booming economy. Toyota Marilao extends its advocacy from ecology to humanity by striving to contribute significantly in creating more jobs and business opportunities for Bulakeños and others living in nearby towns.

Toyota Marilao Bulacan Inc. is operating under SEC Company Registration Number CS201207525 dated April 20, 2012.
